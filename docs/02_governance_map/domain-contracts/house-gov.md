# HOUSE-GOV — House Operations Contract

## Purpose

Keep the physical environment from degrading into hidden operational debt.

## Contract

House governance is valid when house maintenance is touched through bounded sessions or explicit routine action.

## Inputs

- House checklist
- Cleaning route
- Laundry state
- Kitchen observation
- Bathroom observation
- Local friction

## Outputs

- Improved zone
- Trash/laundry/kitchen/bathroom action
- Visible reset
- Deferred next house item

## Tick valid when

A real physical zone or routine improved within a bounded session.

## Stop rule

A CLN-30 tick is thirty minutes or one bounded zone, not a full-house perfection attempt.
