# WEEK-GOV — Weekly Governance Contract

## Purpose

Govern the week as a surface of time, roles, outputs, blockers, routines, and continuity.

The weekly board is not the full operating system. It is the dispatch surface that decides what gets activated this week.

## Contract

A week is governed when:

- active fronts are visible;
- day roles are respected;
- 1-3 visible outputs are named;
- homeostasis and maintenance are not invisible;
- externalization has at least one possible slot;
- Friday or Sunday leaves a bridge to Monday;
- the board remains usable on paper.

## Inputs

- Weekly board
- Previous weekly packet
- Carry State
- Active fronts
- Known blockers
- 14-day cycle status, if active
- Quarterly/seasonal frame, if under review

## Outputs

- Weekly orientation
- Active fronts
- Visible outputs
- Blockers / not-now list
- Monday bridge
- Routes to activate this week

## Tick valid when

The week has a readable shape and the first serious block can be selected without reconstructing context.

## Stop rule

Do not turn weekly governance into full quarterly planning unless that is the explicit scheduled agenda.
