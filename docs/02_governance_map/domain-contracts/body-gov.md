# BODY-GOV — Body / Health Contract

## Purpose

Keep basic body signals visible enough to support decisions.

This is not medical analysis. It is operational sensing.

## Contract

Body governance is valid when core signals are recorded or deliberately marked as missing.

## Inputs

- Body log
- Weight / measures
- Sleep and wake time
- Alcohol signal
- Symptoms or fatigue
- Gym recovery signal

## Outputs

- Logged measure
- Missing marker
- Trend note
- Recovery constraint
- Follow-up if needed

## Tick valid when

A minimum useful body signal was recorded or explicitly marked unavailable.

## Stop rule

Do not convert body logging into open-ended medical research during routine governance.
