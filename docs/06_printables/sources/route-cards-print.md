# Core Route Cards — Printable Deck

---

## BOOT

When: morning / M1.  
Purpose: select a runnable block.  
Output: one block with expected output and boundary.  
Stop rule: do not debug the full system.

---

## MAINT

When: M1 / M2 / M3.  
Purpose: reduce friction and prepare execution.  
Output: repaired context, closed loop, prepared block, or parked item.  
Stop rule: no open-ended refactor or deep rebuild.

---

## FOCUS

When: focus blocks.  
Purpose: produce evidence.  
Output: draft, commit, report, message, deployment, state update, or next pointer.  
Stop rule: do not redesign the whole system inside a focus block.

---

## CLOSE

When: end of day.  
Purpose: prevent context loss.  
Output: smallest useful next pointer.  
Stop rule: no full weekly review.

---

## WEEK-GOV

When: Sunday/Monday or weekly anchor.  
Purpose: orient the week.  
Output: active fronts, routes, blockers, outputs.  
Stop rule: do not become quarterly planning unless scheduled.

---

## CARRY-W

When: Friday or Sunday.  
Purpose: reconcile active project state.  
Output: active fronts with status and next pointer.  
Stop rule: do not re-plan the full project universe.

---

## FIN-W

When: Friday and/or Sunday-Monday.  
Purpose: prevent missed finance obligations.  
Output: paid, registered, replied, deferred, or no-action-needed.  
Stop rule: no deep finance analysis unless blocked.

---

## GYM-GOV

When: gym/movement block.  
Purpose: keep training governed.  
Output: session, recovery decision, or minimal log.  
Stop rule: do not redesign the training plan during workout.

---

## HOUSE / CLN-30

When: maintenance or Saturday.  
Purpose: prevent physical environment drift.  
Output: one improved zone or routine.  
Stop rule: 20-30 minutes or one bounded zone.

---

## EXTERNAL

When: Thursday or as needed.  
Purpose: change an external surface.  
Output: sent, published, contacted, delivered, or feedback requested.  
Stop rule: do not polish instead of sending.

---

## CAPTURE-GOV

When: inside review/capture session.  
Purpose: route free notes.  
Output: action, state, archive, waiting, or discard.  
Stop rule: do not process capture on the weekly board.
