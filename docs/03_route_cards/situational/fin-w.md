
# FIN-W — Weekly Finance Review

## When

Use Friday close and/or Sunday-Monday bridge.

## Purpose

Prevent missed vencimientos, unregistered payments, forgotten claims, and ledger drift.

## Inputs

* Finance calendar
* Ledger
* Finance weekly checklist
* Bills/reminders/messages
* Pending payments or claims

## Steps minimum

1. Check upcoming vencimientos.
2. Check payments/reclaims pending.
3. Register or mark changes.
4. Decide next action for unclear items.
5. Leave a short finance next pointer if needed.

## Output

One of:

* no finance action needed;
* payments completed;
* items registered;
* claim/reply sent;
* next finance actions listed.

## Tick valid when

No near-term financial obligation remains ambiguous.

## Stop rule

Do not open deep financial analysis unless there is a concrete blocker.
