# POLICY-CONTENT-GOV — Policy / Public Content Governance

## When

Use when policy, politics, public argument, or media-facing content is active.

## Purpose

Convert accumulated material into publishable or shareable pieces.

## Inputs

- Notes / research
- Argument library
- Drafts
- Contact/publication target
- Externalization route

## Steps minimum

1. Select one argument or piece.
2. Define audience and format.
3. Produce a sendable/publishable version.
4. Externalize or leave next pointer.
5. Log evidence if useful.

## Output

Public-facing piece advanced or sent.

## Tick valid when

A piece becomes more publishable, more shareable, or actually externalized.

## Stop rule

Do not reopen the full political theory stack while preparing one piece.
