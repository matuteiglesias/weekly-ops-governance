# JOB-GOV — Job Search Governance

## When

Use when job search is an active seasonal or weekly front.

## Purpose

Keep employment pipeline work external, measurable, and response-oriented.

## Inputs

- Job pipeline
- Recruiter messages
- CV / LinkedIn
- Role targets
- Applications or follow-ups

## Steps minimum

1. Check active opportunities.
2. Choose one pipeline movement.
3. Send/apply/respond/follow up.
4. Update state.
5. Leave next pointer.

## Output

Job pipeline advanced.

## Tick valid when

At least one opportunity changed state or one recruiter/application loop advanced.

## Stop rule

Do not rebuild the whole career narrative during routine pipeline work.
