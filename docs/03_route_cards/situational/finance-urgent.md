# FIN-U — Urgent Finance Item

## When

Use when a specific financial item is time-sensitive.

## Purpose

Resolve one urgent financial obligation without opening the full finance system.

## Inputs

- Specific bill, reminder, claim, or payment issue
- Account/payment method
- Ledger or finance state if relevant
- Due date

## Steps minimum

1. Identify the exact item.
2. Decide pay/register/reply/claim/defer.
3. Execute the action.
4. Save confirmation or next pointer.
5. Stop.

## Output

Urgent finance item resolved or explicitly deferred.

## Tick valid when

The item has a concrete outcome.

## Stop rule

Only resolve the urgent item. Do not open deep finance analysis.
