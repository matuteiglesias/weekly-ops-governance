# BODY-GOV — Body / Health Governance

## When

Use in the morning, during weekly review, or when body signals affect execution.

## Purpose

Keep body signals visible without turning them into a research spiral.

## Inputs

- Body log
- Weight/measurements
- Sleep and wake time
- Alcohol signal
- Symptoms/fatigue
- Gym recovery signal

## Steps minimum

1. Record minimum available signal.
2. Mark missing if not available.
3. Notice relevant trend or constraint.
4. Route any health follow-up if needed.
5. Return to normal operation.

## Output

A minimal body state update.

## Tick valid when

One useful signal is recorded or explicitly marked missing.

## Stop rule

Do not do open-ended medical research during routine logging.
