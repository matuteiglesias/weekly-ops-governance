# FOOD-ALCOHOL — Food / Alcohol Signal

## When

Use during BODY-GOV or weekly review when food/alcohol materially affects energy, health, or recovery.

## Purpose

Keep a coarse signal without creating heavy tracking overhead.

## Inputs

- Food notes
- Alcohol signal
- Body log
- Sleep/recovery context

## Steps minimum

1. Record alcohol yes/no or amount if useful.
2. Record only notable food pattern if relevant.
3. Link to body/sleep/gym effect if obvious.
4. Stop.

## Output

Coarse food/alcohol signal.

## Tick valid when

The week has enough signal to interpret major body/recovery changes.

## Stop rule

Do not turn this into full nutrition tracking unless explicitly planned.
