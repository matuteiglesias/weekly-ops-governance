
# GYM-GOV — Gym / Movement Governance

## When

Use before or during gym/movement blocks, usually in the 20-22 buffer or planned training windows.

## Purpose

Make gym blocks governed by routine, progression, recovery, and minimal logging.

## Inputs

* Current routine
* Gym log
* Sleep/fatigue/pain signal
* Available equipment
* Time available

## Steps minimum

1. Identify the routine for today.
2. Check recovery constraints.
3. Execute session or choose recovery deliberately.
4. Log one minimal signal.
5. Avoid redesigning the full program.

## Output

* Session completed;
* recovery decision;
* minimal log;
* progression signal;
* constraint noted.

## Tick valid when

The session followed a plan, or recovery was chosen explicitly rather than by drift.

## Stop rule

Do not redesign the training plan during the workout.
